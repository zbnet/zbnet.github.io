about me

I am a person with a web page and a url that is also my name, which is to say that I am basically a product and you are a potential customer. By which I mean I'm giving a bunch of words away for no money in hope of earning the affection of internet strangers.

My business model is a work in progress.

If you like what I have written, please let me know. If you hate what I have written, please let me know.
